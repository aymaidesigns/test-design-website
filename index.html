BFA Prototype

This was for an assignment for art 102 and we were told to make a space in a box out of 3D models. The process I took into making this was during a time when I started worrying about graduation and doing a BFA show. At the time I did not think of merging the two ideas together, but it was only when I saw a video of pepkura did I start coming up with this idea and eventually made an artwork of a vision for my BFA show.

Make your own Pokemon Starter

This was an interesting process on how this piece came to be. I was in extreme amount of stress because it was going to be my final project for coding class and I had no idea what I should do or make. It then just hit me that I should allow people to make what they wanted, but limited to Pokemon. So then I made a code that draws all the pokemon starters face features and you can draw the outline. Ideally to help people stuck in a block and look at things differently.
